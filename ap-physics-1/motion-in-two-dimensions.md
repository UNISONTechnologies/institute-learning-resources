## Two-Dimensional Formulas
- X: \(x = x_{0} + v_{0xt} + \frac{1}{2}a_{x}t^{2}\)
- Y: \(x = y_{0} + v_{0yt} + \frac{1}{2}a_{y}t^{2}\)
- X: \(v_{x} = v_{0x} + a_{x}t\)
- Y: \(v_{y} = v_{0y} + a_{y}t\)
- X: \({v_{x}}^{2} = {v_{0x}}^{2} + 2a_{x}(x - x_{0})\)
- Y: \({v_{y}}^{2} = {v_{0y}}^{2} + 2a_{y}(y - y_{0})\)
